About Food Additives

  1. Q1What is an Food Additives?

    Food additives prevent food from changing or decaying. Typical are preservatives and antioxidants. Preservatives inhibit the growth of microorganisms while storing food to prevent food from decaying, and antioxidants prevent or slow down oxidation of foods containing oil to prevent quality degradation and extend the storage period.
    Another role is to ensure that the two substances, such as oil and water, are well blended without separation. And it is used to enhance taste, color and smell.

  2. Q2Are all food additives bad?

    Not all food additives are dangerous. There are also naturally derived food additives. For example, propionic acid is produced in the fermentation and ripening process of various microorganisms. It is mainly produced from soy sauce, cheese, vinegar, etc. Foods are added as food additives such as bread and jam.

  3. Q3What food additives are bad?

    There are many different types, but beware of the commonly used food additives Flavor Enhancer, Coloring Agent, Sweeterner, Bleach, Colourant, Preservative, Emulsifier, Leaving Agent.
